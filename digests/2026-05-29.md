The thread is split between criticizing the researcher's erratic behavior and condemning Microsoft for using its platform ownership to silence a critic and undermine the integrity of its bounty program.

### [Blue Origin's New Glenn blows up during static fire test](https://news.ycombinator.com/item?id=48317774)
A static fire test of Blue Origin's New Glenn rocket ended in a massive explosion that destroyed the launch pad and transporter-erector. Commenters note this is a crushing setback that will likely ground the company for over a year while infrastructure is rebuilt, further cementing SpaceX's dominance.
