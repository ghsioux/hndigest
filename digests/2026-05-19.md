## Top Stories

### [Elon Musk has lost his lawsuit against Sam Altman and OpenAI](https://news.ycombinator.com/item?id=48182754)
A jury dismissed Musk's lawsuit against OpenAI on a statute of limitations technicality, finding he waited too long to bring his claims. The HN discussion highlighted the irony of a multi-billion dollar lawsuit failing over a calendar check, though many noted the trial successfully aired out years of internal OpenAI drama and embarrassing emails.

### [Anthropic acquires Stainless](https://news.ycombinator.com/item?id=48182281)
Anthropic acquired the API SDK generator Stainless and is immediately winding down its hosted products. The acquisition sparked frustration in the comments over a growing trend of AI giants acquihiring useful developer tools and taking them off the open market, leaving existing customers scrambling to migrate.

### [We stopped AI bot spam in our GitHub repo using Git's –author flag](https://news.ycombinator.com/item?id=48181125)
An AI company successfully blocked a flood of LLM-generated pull requests by requiring a captcha that creates a tiny co-authored commit, leveraging GitHub's "require prior contribution" setting. Commenters appreciated the clever hack but pointed out the irony of an AI company fighting AI slop, noting that GitHub desperately needs native protections against automated PR spam.
