## Top Stories

### [For first time, a cell built from scratch grows and divides](https://news.ycombinator.com/item?id=48747304)
Katarzyna Adamala's team at Minnesota built minimal synthetic cells, informally called SpudCells, capable of both growing and dividing, closing a gap that had stalled synthetic biology for years after researchers separately achieved DNA replication and basic feeding. The 274-comment thread praised the milestone with caveats: the researchers themselves stop short of claiming they created life, and Science News reported peers grumbling that Cell had previously rejected the work because one reviewer said SpudCells "are not real biology."

### [ZCode – Harness for GLM-5.2](https://news.ycombinator.com/item?id=48753715)
Z.ai released ZCode, a desktop coding-agent harness for its GLM-5.2 model, to 280 comments dominated by one theme: trust. Developer after developer described running coding agents exclusively inside VMs or containers to avoid giving any agent direct access to their host machine, and several linked their own isolation tooling. The product is also closed source, which multiple readers found hard to swallow given open-source CLI alternatives doing similar work.

### [Monetization Gateway: Charge for any resource behind Cloudflare via x402](https://news.ycombinator.com/item?id=48746914)
Cloudflare launched a monetization layer for any proxied resource using the HTTP 402 payment protocol, enabling per-request billing for APIs, files, or pages without custom payment integration. Readers were broadly skeptical: the product handles agent-to-API billing reasonably well, but does nothing for the real pain point, which is bot traffic driving up hosting costs for content sites where asking humans to pay to browse is a non-starter.
