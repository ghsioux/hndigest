## Top Stories

### [Qwen 3.6 27B is the sweet spot for local development](https://news.ycombinator.com/item?id=48721903)
Quesma argues that Qwen 3.6's 27B parameter build now delivers the best practical cost-to-quality balance for local development inference. The 594-comment thread evolved into hardware advice: M5 MacBook Pro owners documented heat and fan noise as genuine daily-use problems, and multiple engineers independently recommended the Mac Mini M4 with 64 GB RAM over LAN as a cheaper, quieter, and thermally sane alternative for inference workloads.

### [US Supreme Court rules geofence warrants require constitutional protections](https://news.ycombinator.com/item?id=48720924)
The Supreme Court ruled that geofence warrants must meet Fourth Amendment probable cause and particularity standards, in a case where Google progressively narrowed 19 anonymous device accounts to three named bank robbery suspects. Readers were cautiously positive but skeptical: many noted that "probable cause" in practice means a rubber-stamped warrant, not a meaningful barrier to the same mass-identification sweep.

### [.self: A new top-level domain designed to support self-hosting](https://news.ycombinator.com/item?id=48724230)
The Human Centered Cloud Foundation is proposing a .self TLD for home servers and self-hosted services, aiming to provide stable addresses without commercial domain registration costs. The 269-comment thread surfaced immediate skepticism: the .tk TLD's collapse into a spam sink is the canonical counterexample, and a deeper argument ran throughout: any single-registry TLD reintroduces the central dependency self-hosting is meant to circumvent.

### [Rocketlab acquires Iridium](https://news.ycombinator.com/item?id=48719485)
Rocket Lab announced an agreement to acquire Iridium Communications, combining Rocket Lab's launch operations with Iridium's 66-satellite LEO constellation, global L-band spectrum, and established position in satellite messaging and IoT services. The deal creates a fully vertically integrated satellite company spanning launch manufacturing, satellite operations, and existing orbital infrastructure.
