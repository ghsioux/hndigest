### [We found an undocumented bug in the Apollo 11 guidance computer code](https://news.ycombinator.com/item?id=47673005)
Researchers used a modern specification language to model the Apollo 11 guidance computer and discovered an undocumented bug in the gyro lock acquisition. It is a fascinating look at how software that ran on 4KB of memory and got humans to the moon still hides complex failure modes.
