### [Go hard on agents, not on your filesystem](https://news.ycombinator.com/item?id=47550282)
A Stanford researcher released `jai`, a lightweight Linux container system designed to sandbox AI agents and prevent them from wiping local filesystems. The thread highlights a fascinating industry tension: developers are recklessly giving hallucination-prone agents full system access because the productivity gains are simply too good to pass up, despite the obvious security nightmare.
