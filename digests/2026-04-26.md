### [Amateur armed with ChatGPT solves an Erdős problem](https://news.ycombinator.com/item?id=47903126)
A 23-year-old with no advanced math training used ChatGPT to solve a 60-year-old Erdős problem. By letting the model run for 80 minutes using "extended thinking," it found a novel application of an existing formula. The HN discussion is a fascinating debate on whether this represents true reasoning or just the sheer combinatorial power of applying techniques across domains faster than humans can.

### [New 10 GbE USB adapters are cooler, smaller, cheaper](https://news.ycombinator.com/item?id=47899053)
A look at the new RTL8159-based 10 GbE USB adapters that finally solve the thermal and size issues of previous generations. While the hardware is an improvement, the HN thread dives deep into the nuances of USB 3.2 overhead versus Thunderbolt's native PCIe tunneling, noting that you still need Thunderbolt to reliably hit full 10 Gbps speeds on a Mac.

### [Tell HN: An app is silently installing itself on my iPhone every day](https://news.ycombinator.com/item?id=47906253)
Multiple users are reporting that the "Headspace" app is silently installing itself on their personal iPhones without permission, even with automatic downloads disabled and no MDM profiles. Speculation in the comments points to a bizarre iOS bug, possibly related to offloaded apps or rogue local notifications triggering a reinstall loop.
