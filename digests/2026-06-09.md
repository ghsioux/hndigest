### [Apple reveals new AI architecture built around Google Gemini models](https://news.ycombinator.com/item?id=48450142)

An analysis of xAI's move to lease its massive Colossus GPU cluster to Anthropic and Google. Commenters debate whether this signals xAI dropping out of the AGI race to become an infrastructure provider, or if it is just a pragmatic way to recoup capital expenditures while the AI compute shortage persists.

The community discussion highlights the technical impossibility of private age verification and the creeping normalization of device-level surveillance under the guise of child protection.
